About Us

We are a part of the Manipal Education and Medical Group (MEMG), a leader in the areas of education and healthcare. With more than 5000 operational beds, our commitment to the overall well-being of an individual is at the core of everything we do. Through our network of hospitals and experienced team of medical professionals, we provide quality and affordable healthcare to everyone
